My ongoing thesis is research on the development of a state-of-the-art unsupervised end-to-end deep learning method for Strain Elastography (SE) through the estimation of the displacement field (similar to the estimation of optical flow in computer vision). SE is a medical imaging technique that determines the tissue region strain, which helps detect/classify the tumor/lesion region from the surrounding tissue based on their strain.

Progress:

  • Implemented ReUSENet paper in Pytorch.
  • Developed a model consisting of a new encoder with TriCrossAttention, Convolutional-LSTM based decoder, and a novel loss for Displacement Field Estimation in Strain Elastography from RF sequence, and implemented this in Pytorch.
  • My proposed model achieves results close to SOTA and beats the ReUSENet (implemented) in terms of SNR, CNR, and NRMSE metrics.
